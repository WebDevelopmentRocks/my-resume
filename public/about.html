About Me

Stefan Peinsipp has been working in a real estate company since its establishment, not only overseeing the entire marketing department but also constantly developing new ideas to facilitate the customer's search and decision-making process for purchasing.

After many diverse years in tourism and event management, where he traveled around the world working with international clients, he began training as an asset and real estate advisor and has been very successful in this industry ever since.

In 2006, he founded his first startup in the technology sector. He applies his accumulated know-how and creative ideas to the development of digital process implementations in various fields.
